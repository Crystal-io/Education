## 🔥 Key Focus Areas (Critical for Success)

### 1. Brand Launch Ownership
- Understand full lifecycle:
  - Market → Concept → Delivery → Launch → Optimization
- Be able to explain real scenarios (e.g., launching casino in Germany)

---

### 2. Ownership Mindset
- Replace:
  - “I participated” → “I owned / I drove”
- Show responsibility for outcomes, not only tasks

---

### 3. Decision Making
- Be able to:
  - Prioritize features
  - Make trade-offs
  - Justify decisions

---

### 4. Product Thinking
- Focus on:
  - User value
  - Business impact
- Not just system logic

---

- Weak emphasis on delivery responsibility
